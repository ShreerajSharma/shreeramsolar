Our Story

Shaping Solar in Rajasthan

We started with a single mission: to make clean, sustainable solar electricity accessible, affordable, and worry-free for every rooftop in Rajasthan.

Core Purpose

Empowering Rooftops with Sunlight

Based in Jaipur, Shreeram Solar has grown into a leading solar installer. We manage the entire lifecycle of solar installations—from structural engineering design, net-metering liaisoning, state and central subsidy approvals, to periodic maintenance checkups.
