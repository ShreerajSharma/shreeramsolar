Workflow

Our Project Deployment Steps

We follow a rigorous, 4-step deployment cycle to ensure absolute safety, correct structural engineering, and fast net-metering synchronization.

01

Technical Roof Mapping

Our structural analysts visit your site to measure angles, load capacity, and trace shadow obstructions to compute the optimal capacity size.

02

Bespoke Layout CAD

We generate clean engineering drawings in CAD to optimize panel placement for optimal solar radiation collection during winter and summer.

03

Licensing & Approvals

We coordinate with state DISCOM agencies for net-metering feasibility studies, permissions, and government subsidy registration paperwork.

04

Deployment & Synch

Our certified technicians install the structures, run cabling, configure earthing lines, and coordinate the final grid net-meter testing.
